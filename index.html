Problem & Solution

The Problem

Despite Bitcoin's $1.2 trillion market cap, over 99% of BTC sits idle with no protection or yield. The Bitcoin ecosystem lacks:

  • Native insurance standards for wallets or transactions
  • Wallet security and behavioral risk detection tools for retail users
  • Compliance-ready infrastructure for institutional adoption
  • AI-powered solutions to forecast market events or detect anomalies
  • Proactive protection for Layer 2 and BTCFi environments

This infrastructure gap leaves Bitcoin holders vulnerable to billions in annual losses from hacks, scams, and smart contract failures.

Our Solution

BlocentraAI is building the first AI-native, modular risk infrastructure platform designed specifically for Bitcoin:

  • Dynamic, behavior-driven insurance policies for all Bitcoin use cases
  • Real-time risk intelligence engine with wallet scoring and anomaly detection
  • Predictive AI models to anticipate systemic risks and black swan events
  • Non-custodial protection that preserves user ownership
  • Institutional-grade compliance dashboards and reporting
  • Secure yield activation for idle Bitcoin through AI-vetted BTCFi protocols

We're creating the trust layer Bitcoin needs for mainstream adoption.
