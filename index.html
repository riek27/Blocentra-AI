Frequently Asked Questions

Is BlocentraAI custodial?
No — our architecture emphasizes non-custodial protections; you keep control of private keys while policies cover specific risks and insured vault flows.
When will the product be available?
We are targeting phased launches with wider availability following the MVP. Waitlist members get priority invites. (See MVP demo for feature coverage.)
How do claims work?
Claims are evaluated via our claims management system — fast triage, forensic analysis, and payout rails for validated losses. Institutional partners can request SLAs.
Which countries will you support?
We plan phased market entries — initially US and EU, expanding globally while securing necessary licenses.
